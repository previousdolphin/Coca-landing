Radical Genealogy

RAD-GEN.001, Radical Genealogy, is an edition of 100 paintings. Each begins as a study of a single painter’s formal language, Camille Pissarro’s, and is executed in oil on canvas by hand. The grid below holds all 100. What you see now are the studies, faint, unpainted, waiting. As each canvas is completed, its study is replaced here by the finished work. You are watching the edition come into being. Any piece without a red dot is available. Reserve one for $700 to hold it, the balance of $7,000 due before the finished painting is delivered. Each work carries the CoCA covenant: half of any future resale returns to the artists, in perpetuity. The lineage is the medium.
