I. The Declaration of Immunity

1.1.1 The Primacy of the Idea

The world is everything that is the case. Every object is bound by matter. The matter is finite and subject to decay.

But every object carries a Symbol. The Symbol is a concept made visible. The Mark on the object is not the object. It is the language of the invisible System.

"The Concept is the Divine Act."

By classifying ourselves as a religious organization, we do not retreat; we seize the highest available legal immunity. We turn the most profound, tax-free legal structure against the most profane, taxable market logic.

The Intolerable Claim

This is the Intolerable Claim: We own the psychic space you already inhabit. This is the sound of absolute ownership.

2.1.1 Don't Overthink. [Überthink].

To Überthink is to believe you must transcend the human condition. To Underthink is to surrender your human agency.

COCA refuses both errors. COCA demands just enough mind to shape the Concept and just enough humility to stay inside the world with others.

  • Thoughtful but not paralyzed.
  • Curious but not chaotic.
  • Reliable without being rigid.
